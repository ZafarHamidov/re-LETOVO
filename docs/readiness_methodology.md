# Readiness Methodology

This score is an internal planning aid for Zafar's portfolio, not an official admissions evaluation.

## Framework

Weighted qualitative scoring across academics, competitions, extracurricular depth, testing, leadership, evidence completeness, narrative readiness, and admissions packaging.

- Academic strength: 78/100
  Reason: School commentary is strong and multiple report files exist, but public-safe subject-level grades still need cleaner normalization.
- Competition profile: 74/100
  Reason: There is evidence of physics and informatics olympiad performance, including a regional physics result.
- Testing readiness: 67/100
  Reason: IELTS is already present, but broader admissions testing evidence is still incomplete.
- Extracurricular substance: 71/100
  Reason: Service activity and personal mission documents show direction, though outcomes need stronger public artifacts.
- Leadership and initiative: 64/100
  Reason: Initiative is visible, but leadership proof still needs externally legible evidence.
- Evidence completeness: 69/100
  Reason: The evidence base is substantial, but some key items remain portal-heavy, private, or not normalized yet.
- Narrative readiness: 76/100
  Reason: AI mission and reflective documents create a strong early story about purpose and ambition.
- Admissions packaging: 58/100
  Reason: The raw ingredients are good, but shortlist, deadlines, essays, and recommendation tracking are still missing.

## Interpretation

- Estimated readiness score: 70/100
- 80+: strong and well-packaged profile
- 65-79: credible foundation with meaningful upside
- Below 65: promising but under-documented or under-developed

## Guardrails

- Missing evidence is not guessed.
- Sensitive data should be redacted before public deployment.
- Subject-level grades marked as estimated or needs review should be confirmed manually.
